VICTORIAN COUNCILS — BLUE-GREEN ALGAE WARNINGS
==============================================
All 79 Victorian councils were checked. Most do NOT issue their own
warnings — for the majority of lakes and reservoirs the warning comes from
a water corporation (Goulburn-Murray Water, GWMWater, Lower Murray Water,
Southern Rural Water, Coliban Water, Barwon Water) rather than the council.

The councils below DO issue their own warnings for lakes they manage. Their
lakes are pre-loaded into the scraper's GEOCODE table, so a news mention
pins accurately. (Confidence noted where the warning was seen directly vs.
inferred from a council page.)
